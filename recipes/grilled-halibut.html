Grilled Halibut

This recipe has a prep time of 10 minutes, a cook time of 10 minutes, for a total time of 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • ¾ teaspoon dried dill weed (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon white sugar (Optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 inch-thick halibut steaks

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Stir butter, lemon juice, onion powder, parsley, dill, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; spread evenly over the halibut steaks.
  3. Cook on preheated grill until the fish flakes easily with a fork, 5 to 6 minutes per side.
